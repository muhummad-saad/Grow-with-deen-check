From Zero to Independent

A student's journey of finding a way to earn without compromising faith.

I remember sitting in my dorm room, checking my bank balance. It was almost empty. I wanted to help my parents, buy my own books, and just feel independent. But every "make money online" video I saw involved things that didn't sit right with my heart—interest-based crypto, dropshipping junk, or deceptive marketing.

"I wanted income, but more than that, I wanted barakah in my Rizq."

That's when I discovered digital products. Creating value once and selling it to people who actually need it. No shipping, no inventory, and most importantly—honest work.

Grow with Deen isn't a corporation. It's just me, sharing the exact blueprints I used to go from a broke student to earning a halal income online. It requires work, intention, and patience. If you're ready for that, you're in the right place.
